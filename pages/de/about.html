About me

I am a Hungarian Full-Stack Web Developer living in Klosterlechfeld, Germany. I have completed CareerFoundry's Full-Stack Web Development course and am ambitious to learn more about the profession. When developing a website, I strive to work with precision and effectiveness, aiming to create a product that is as user-friendly as possible.

I have been interested in computers since I was a child. I enjoy engaging in activities involving them, with games being my favorite pastime. Over time, I learned how to build and maintain desktop PCs and laptops, a skill I apply for both my family members and myself. This experience sparked my interest in learning how to code, a journey that I thoroughly enjoy. So far, I have more experience building the frontend of applications, but I am eager to gain experience in developing the server-side as well. I am dedicated to continuous learning, and I approach it with consistency, speed, and thoroughness.

During my previous roles, I acquired valuable skills such as teamwork, a customer-centric mindset, and honed my problem-solving abilities. While I tend to be more of an active listener than a talker, I excel in collaborating with colleagues and superiors.

I am open to full-time on-site, hybrid, and remote work.
