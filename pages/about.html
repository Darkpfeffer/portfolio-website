About me

I am a Hungarian full-stack web developer living in Klosterlechfeld, Germany. I am a beginner in this field. When I develop a website, I strive to work with precision and effectiveness. My goal is to have a product as user friendly as possible.

I have an interest in computers since I was a kid. I enjoy the activities I was able to do with them, and I most relish games. I learned how to build and maintain desktop PC-s, and I do it for my family and myself. It led me to the idea of learning how to code. I enjoy every part of it, especially building the frontend. Mostly, I learn consistently, fast and thoroughly. At my former workplaces I learned how to work in a team, to have a customer mindset, and worked on my problem solving skills. I am more of an active listener than an active talker, but I can cooperate with colleagues and superiors well.

I am open to full-time on-site, hybrid, and remote work.
